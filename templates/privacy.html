Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

At Spendly, your privacy matters. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have — in plain language.

1

Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect:

  • Account information — the name, email address, and password you provide when registering. Passwords are stored in hashed form, never as plain text.
  • Expense data — the amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter to track your spending.

Spendly does not connect to your bank or financial institutions and does not collect account numbers, card details, or transaction data from any external source.

2

How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to provide the Service:

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To display your expense history, category breakdowns, and monthly summaries.
  • To maintain and improve the reliability of the Service.

We do not sell your personal or financial data, and we do not use your expense records for advertising.

3

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access. Access to your expense records requires your account credentials, so keep them confidential. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

You can update or delete your expense records at any time from within the app. If you wish to delete your account and its associated data, you may request this through the Service.

4

Third Party Services

Spendly does not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties, except where required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Spendly and its users.

The Service may rely on basic infrastructure providers (such as hosting and font delivery) to operate. These providers do not receive your expense records and are not permitted to use any data for their own purposes.

5

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Spendly after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

6

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the Service — we're happy to help.

Questions?

You may also want to read our Terms and Conditions to understand the rules that govern your use of Spendly.
